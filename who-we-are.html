Our Story

Coker Creative is a boutique experience design company that curates every event into a unique experience for each client. Since our inception in 2014, the award-winning company has curated refined events in West and South Africa, Europe, and North America for private and corporate clients.

Our approach to event experiences is simple. No two clients or event experiences are the same and so we treat them that way by curating detailed experiences that are tailored to each individual client’s desires. We curate experiences in various mediums such as Events. Brand Curation. Destination Experiences

Core Values

Coker Creative’s philosophy is that the journey to every event experience should be as beautiful and as enjoyable as the event itself. Our events are designed to ensure that our clients’ desired outcomes are not only well-executed, but translate into a distinctively tailored experience for every guest in attendance to enjoy.

Our commitment to excellence and being unique is reflected in our philosophy that no two clients are the same, so no events should ever be the same.
